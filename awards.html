Corporate Social Responsibility

We have a variety of sponsorship agreements in place, including sponsoring UK Athletics Jess Turner, Tring Rugby Club, The Old Tonbridgians Rugby Club, Radlett Cricket Club and Sevenoaks Vine Cricket Club.

In return for our sponsorship we offer the club and its members valuable financial advice and to date we have provided support of over £40,000, we hope making a huge difference to local community sport.
