Fees & Compliance

With a philosophy centred on two-way communication and transparency, we will always be upfront and open about what our advice will cost.

We work at an hourly rate for initial planning work and our fees are based on our experience of the time needed to complete your individual piece of work.

Our fees do not automatically increase with investment size.

All relationships start with a conversation, so get in touch with any of our team to find out how Finura Partners can benefit you and your business.

Central to our advice process and value add is our ongoing, regular reviews.

We must ensure that we continue to update your lifestyle plan that our planning remains fit for your objectives.

Most clients prefer us to have a vested interest in their success and thus we charge a percentage based retainer linked to the assets we manage.
