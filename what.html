What we do

Lifestyle planning
and modelling

Lifestyle planning and modelling is about defining and committing your individual aspirations to a visual financial plan. We can test ideas you thought not possible, such as early retirement or we can stress test market conditions.

We can factor in cool fees, increases in earnings or sabbaticals and cautious or motivate divestment strategies. By creating plan and being accountable to it, we can ensure that our solutions and planning are working toward something identifiable and unique rather than simply being an off the shelf solution.

What we do

Behavioural finance
and coaching

As human beings we are all subject to biases and prejudices that are a result of our experience. As psychologists have noted that problem with these biases is that many are unseen and a function of our subconscious. This can be seen most readily in e.g. famous BF experiment. If these biases are unrecognised this will lead to erroneous decision making.

Our role is to help you identify these biases or tendencies and to strip back the media noise and conflicting information to deliver planning that works because it is proven, not due to timing or luck.
